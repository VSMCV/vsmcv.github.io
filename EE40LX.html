EE40LX Online Course

My first course on edx.org. Gained basic knowledge mostly in AC Circuit Analysis, filters, transfer functions, etc. The final goal of the course was to build a light and sound sensitive robot that moves in a rather exotic fashion, using eccentrically wheighted motors to bounce on top of springs. I completed all of the requirements for the robot, which included reacting to clapping, making some sounds, and moving towards light. Processing was done by an MSP430, but the course used an Arduino-like IDE for code.
