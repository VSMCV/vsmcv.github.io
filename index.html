Hello, I'm Vlad.

I'm an Electrical and Computer Engineering junior studying at Cornell. This is the place to find out more about my independent projects and other work outside classes.

I've known I wanted to be an electrical engineer since early high school, and this website captures my progress in the field. From the simpler high school robots to the underwater acoustic systems I'm building now, all of my work has something in common: I've always taken pride in finding elegant solutions. Building a working prototype is just half the fun for me - understanding the problem thoroughly and using the most out of my resources is the prize.

Recently, as I've gained a more mathematical understanding of electrical engineering, digital signal processing has become my primary interest. I believe there is a plethora of knowledge about the world that I can gain through DSP, and all it takes to build a prototype is my computer, which is a very liberating feeling. However, my earlier work shows that I am also adept at designing and assembling hardware, and that I am experienced in firmware development.
